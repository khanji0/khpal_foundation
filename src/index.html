Our Mission

At Khpal Foundation, we ensure no one goes to bed hungry. Through compassion and collective action, we bring hope and nourishment to those in need.

Our Story

Khpal Foundation was born from witnessing hunger's devastating impact across Pakistan. Children without meals, elderly struggling to survive, entire families going days without food. This foundation is my commitment to changing that reality.

Khpal means "self" in Pashtu, embodying our belief that caring for others is caring for ourselves.

Jibran Ahmed Khan

Founder & Director
